What is the goal of this site?

The goal of this website is to collect, store and present weather data for my specific county (Gwinnett) over a period of 10 years. This website aims to provide information on noteworthy weather events, like the last date of snow or hottest recorded temperature. This information can be useful to both tourists and homebuyers while also acting as a local marker for short-term climate change. It should be noted that this is meant only for data collection of weather events and not weather predictions.
