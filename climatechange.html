What does Climate Change look like for Metro-Atlanta?

Climate Change is having a vastly negative impact on not only the world or Georgia but on our local municipalities as well. The number of days where Metro-Atlanta is over 100 degrees Fahrenheit is expected to double by 2053 along with an ever-growing risk of wildfires every year
